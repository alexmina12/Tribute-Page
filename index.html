Fiscal Paradise.


History:

  • 1841
    - A smallpox epidemic broke out in the Bur Dubai locality, forcing residents to relocate east to Deira.
  • 1882
    - A British naval surveyor noted that Dubai was at that time populated with a thousand people living in an oval-shaped town surrounded by a mud wall, scattered with goats and camels.
  • 1883
    - following tribal feuding, members of the Al Bu Falasah tribe seceded from Abu Dhabi and established themselves in Dubai.
  • 1892
    - Dubai also - like its neighbours on the Trucial Coast - entered into an exclusivity agreement in which the United Kingdom took responsibility for the emirate's security.
  • 1896
    - fire broke out in Dubai, a disastrous occurrence in a town where many family homes were still constructed from barasti - palm fronds.
